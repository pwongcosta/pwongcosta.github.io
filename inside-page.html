Background

I am a young (twenty year old) multipotentialite designer from San Francisco and Marin County. Yes, my wallet is still crying. I am currently residing in Davis while I finish up a double major undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Design with an emphasis on Immersive Interactive Media Environments and Human Computer Interaction Studies. In the future, I plan on taking my career into designing immersive environments and experiences for the masses with a focus on creating functional and robust platforms designed around interactive user experiences.

Extracurriculars

Outside of my profession, I backpack around the world and am an avid bookworm (I've recently got really into podcasts for my long car rides). I teach math and science part time for kindergarteners at Pioneer Elementary School, and have been volunteering as a youth outreach counselor for California Youth Outreach initiative for five years plus.
